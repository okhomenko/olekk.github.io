# About

I work on engineering systems that improve leverage: architecture boundaries, AI-assisted development, developer infrastructure, and the operational mechanics that determine whether software teams get faster or slower over time.

Most of the writing here focuses on one question:

```text
How do you increase execution speed without increasing entropy?
```

That usually turns into discussions about:

- architecture and cost of change
- development infrastructure
- review economics
- AI execution workflows
- ownership boundaries
- operational simplicity
- developer experience under real constraints

I prefer systems that are:

- understandable
- composable
- operationally boring
- easy to recover
- difficult to misuse accidentally

This site intentionally stays simple:

```text
plain HTML
minimal JavaScript
durable links
small surface area
```

The goal is clarity, not platform theater.
